Dog Breed ID
This is a simple app that lets the user identify the breed of a dog by either taking a picture, or by selecting an image from their image gallery. The app is using a pre-trained convolutional network that has been retrained with techniques such as transfer learning and fine-tunning. Enjoy the app!
